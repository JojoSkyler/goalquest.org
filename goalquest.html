INTRODUCTION

GOAL QUEST FOUNDATION is a Community Based Organization committed to sustainability, social changes & youth empowerment. We are devoted to engaging the youths in matters of environmental responsibility. We aim to empower young minds with the knowledge and tools necessary to become responsible environmental stewards.

OUR PILLARS

1. Youth Empowerment:

-Empowering the next generation through education, mentorship and skill development.
-Creating opportunities for young individuals to unlock their full potential and contribute to their communities.

2. Environmental Conservation:

-Initiatives focused on sustainable practices, reforestation and reducing our ecological footprint.
-Collaborating with communities to raise awareness and implement eco-friendly solutions.

3. Mental Health Awareness:

-Breaking the stigma surrounding mental health through advocacy and educational programs.
-Providing resources and support for individuals facing mental health challenges.

OUR MISSION


To inspire people to enhance health and overcome hunger and hardships.

OUR VISION


To empower a world to which the less fortunate will have the power to uplift themselves out of poverty.

CATALOGUE

EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE


CONSOLATA SHRINE


Goal Quest Foundation in close partnership with Felicia Mariga Foundation, successfully organized a transformative event at Consolata Shrine, mentoring girls on menstrual hygiene. We also distributed essential sanitary pads, contributing to the well-being of these young individuals. Together, we strive to empower and uplift communities.


CLEAN UP INITIATIVE


We successfully completed a clean up event in partnership with the Nairobi County Government, Felicia Mariga Organization and key stakeholders within the ward on March 8th.


BRILLIANT ACADEMY


We successfully commenced our long-planned mentorship program at Brilliant academy with our representatives initiating insightful teachings for the students. We embraced open dialogue among the Brilliant Angels. The program is structured to occur twice every month on Fridays. Looking forward to the continued success.


CLIMATE SECURITY FORUM


We gather at the Climate Security Forum to forge a path towards a resilient, sustainable and secure future.


ALLIANCE HIGHSCHOOL


On February 10th, we successfully completed the follow-up on the trees we planted on 2023 World Environment in partnership with Alliance Highschool.We replaced 100 seedlings which had failed.


KIRITI GIRLS MENSTRUAL HYGIENE


We organized a football tournament at Kiriti Girls' School in Murang'a County, integrating menstrual hygiene education into the event. Through mentorship and guidance during sports activities, we empowered 400 girls with essential knowledge about menstrual hygiene. By merging sports and education, we fostered a supportive environment for open dialogue, breaking down stigmas and promoting healthier practices among young women.

ALLIANCE TREE PLANTING


On June 10, 2023, World Environment Day witnessed a remarkable initiative as Goal Quest Foundation in collaboration with Alliance High School, Global Peace Foundation and various organizations, planted 2000 trees. This successful event highlights the critical importance of environmental conservation through tree planting. Trees play a pivotal role in combating climate change by absorbing carbon(iv)oxide, purifying the air, preventing soil erosion and providing habitat for diverse species. Such concerted efforts underscore the urgent need for collective action to preserve our environment and ensure a sustainable future for generations to come.
